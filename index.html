KEY DATA

Douar Tech Association aims to promote the economic independence of young people from precarious backgrounds in rural Morocco through entrepreneurship training. Its mission is to encourage and help these young people either to access a first job or to become self-employed entrepreneurs or leaders of TPE.

Beneficiaries

20 beneficiaries in 3 douars close to Sidi Yahya Zaër in Tamesna, Morocco.

Volunteers

20 professionals, from diverse backgrounds, volunteering for Douar Tech.

Goal for Prototype

20 micro-businesses/SMEs tech leveraged in industries related to the rural economy.

OUR APPROACH

Douar Tech is building genuine high-quality, relevant, and engaging content in a variety of forms.

  • Blended learning models

    combine face-to-face instruction in workshops, mentorship by professionals and online learning activities for its target population that has basic if no digital knowledge

  • Flexible structure

    Class time and structure will be flexible,
    to adapt to the learning needs of the students.

  • Tailored learning

    Students will have access to multiple sources of instruction and use assessment and diagnostic tools to help direct the pace and format of their learning.

  • Hands-on approach

    We hope to empower the youth we’re addressing, foster entrepreneurship and accelerate the creation of sustainable SMEs leveraged by tech in fields related to the rural economy.

We aim at empowering the precarious youth of rural Morocco with business and digital literacy skills.
